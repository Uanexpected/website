About us

Crafted by hand,
finished with care

Glow Collective began with a simple belief that great makeup isn't about hiding a face, it's about revealing the best version of it. Based in Dharampur, Dehradun, our studio has grown into a trusted name for festive, celebratory, and everyday-glam looks across the city.

Every artist on our team trains continuously in modern techniques HD, airbrush, draping, and traditional finishes so your look feels right for the moment, whether that's a family function, a friend's celebration, or a night out. We work only with premium, branded, skin-friendly products, because what goes on your skin matters as much as how it looks on camera.

Our studio is built around one idea: you walk in as yourself, and walk out as the most confident version of yourself.
